The tourist destinations in Kashmir

There are many tourist destinations in kashmir, but here are some of the tourist spots that hold a lot of presence in the eye of locals and tourists.

Pahalgam:

The tourist site Pahalgam is located in the district of Anantnag, Kashmir. Its a hill station that holds some of the majestic scenary that world could offer. In Pahalgam there are places such as Betab Valley, Chandanwari, Baisaran, Aru Valley. There are major lakes that appeal a lot to the tourist and local trekkers, example LidderWatt, TarSar, MarSar and more.
